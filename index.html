• Listomania

    Simple game all about ordering lists. Initially created for the original iPad.

  • Listomania Soccer Edition

    Same as Listomania but all categories are about soccer

  • All lies

    Game prototype. Your quest is to find the lie in a list of hints. If the presented hint is a lie press "q" (player 1) or "p" (player 2). You can also tap on the left or right part of the screen.
    English version: All lies in english

  • Stop it

    Game prototype for the iPad. Your on a raft and your quest is to reach the island. Tap and hold the wholes that are poked in your raft.

  • Circa

    Quiz prototype. It's mix between a guesstimation game and "tic tac toe". Be the first to have three in a row - but you have to earn your spots by answering questions and estimating the answer

  • Word Salad (german)

    Game prototype: Your getting letters for a word of a specific category. Every second one letter is positions automatically in the right position. If you know the word you can drag the letters to the current position - the faster the better. (Reload to get the next word)

  • Was liegt wo?

    Geo-Quiz: It's a quiz about the locations of things and cities. Place your marker on the web and see how good you are. You can even play against others.
